Jamel (20150903) is an open source agent-based framework dedicated to the modeling, the simulation and the analysis of complex monetary economies.

Jamel is currently developped by Dr. P. Seppecher (original author, University of Paris 13 and University of Nice Sophia Antipolis, France), Dr. I. Salle (University of Amsterdam, The Netherlands) and Dr. H. Hagedorn (Business and Information Technology School of Berlin, Germany).

A demo version of Jamel, together with several simulation scenarios, can be downloaded here: http://p.seppecher.free.fr/jamel/download.php.
The source code of Jamel is available here: https://github.com/pseppecher/jamel.
